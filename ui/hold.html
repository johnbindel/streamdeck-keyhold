When you let go, Before release is tapped while the hold is still down, then the hold is released, then After release is tapped. Use either, both, or neither. The pauses sit either side of letting go — raise them if the target app misses a hotkey that lands in the same instant as the release.
Click the field, then press a key combination. Ctrl, Alt/Option, Shift, and Cmd/Win can be recorded by themselves. The combination stays down for exactly as long as you hold the button or pedal.
