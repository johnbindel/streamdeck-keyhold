Click a field, then press a key combination. Ctrl, Alt/Option, Shift, and Cmd/Win can be recorded by themselves. The hold combination stays down while the button or pedal is pressed. When you let go, Before release is tapped while the hold is still down, then the hold is released, then After release is tapped. Use either, both, or neither.
The pauses sit either side of letting go of the hold. Raise them if the target app misses a hotkey that lands in the same instant as the release; leave them at 0 otherwise.
